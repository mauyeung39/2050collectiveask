01 —

How we came to be

Following the 2016 elections, Movement for Black Lives and allied organizations launched Rising Majority — a Black-led, multi-racial formation to build power across issues, sectors, and geographies. Since 2017, it has grown to a national coalition of more than 85 organizations committed to transforming how resources are shared, how we govern, and how we care for one another and the planet.

In 2024, more than 800 activists, organizers, and scholars convened to bridge divides and chart a path forward. From that gathering, 60+ alliances committed to stay united through the 2024 elections and pursue a shared 2050 vision — and that commitment has only grown stronger.

The moment we're in

The Trump administration has brought intensified attacks on our communities and the values that ground our work — mutual care, solidarity, and belonging. Our movement remains siloed and under-resourced even as challenges grow more connected.

We believe that to win, we must coordinate across sectors, pool our leadership and resources, and act with shared purpose and strategy. This crisis is an opportunity to align and strengthen our individual efforts.

02 —

How we'll use the funds

Resources raised will strengthen Rising Majority and member organizations to block and build — defending communities while advancing the offensive vision for what comes next.

Ground game

Base-building & mass organizing

Shared programs of base-building, grassroots campaigns, and organizing between dozens of organizations — building collective identity and infrastructure beyond election cycles.

Co-governance

Divest/Invest campaigns

Praxis tables leading coordination of co-governance initiatives and divest/invest strategies. Practicing democracy at scale and shifting resources toward care and away from harm.

Coordination

Strategic leadership capacity

Capacity for RM's Leadership Team to coordinate in crisis moments, develop strategy as political terrain evolves, and strengthen social movement leadership across sectors.

Narrative

Socializing the 2050 vision

Narrative workers and organizations aligning across sectors to develop mass-facing experiments that bring the 2050 Vision to new bases and audiences.
